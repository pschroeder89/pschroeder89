Intro

Hey, I'm Paul. I am a Principal Software Engineer in Test at Splunk, with experience in developing automation frameworks for both web and mobile UI, as well as APIs. When I'm not working, I also enjoy developing plugins for Homebridge or introducting new features to Python modules I use. My fascination with computers and how they work led me to the software industry, where I've discovered my love for testing software and creating tools. One of these tools was a Selenium Docker image I created that could simulate speech that I showed off at SeleniumConf 2019 in Tokyo, Japan.

I am constantly looking for new ways to expand my knowledge and improve my skills, which mostly revolve around Python, JavaScript, GoLang, and Bash. I am always on the lookout for new projects, whether for work or for personal fulfillment, that will allow me to continue growing as a software engineer. With my dedication and expertise, I am confident in my ability to make a significant impact on any project I undertake.

Outside of coding and testing, you can find me hiking the Colorado mountains, gaming, or vacationing on a beach.
